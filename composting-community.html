COMPOS(T)ING
COMMUNITY

This project explores the creation of a community garden designed to respond to the loss of green spaces caused by urban densification in King’s Cross.

It proposes a shared, accessible landscape that supports community interaction while reintroducing nature into a dense urban environment.

VISION

The proposal creates a shared environment where residents can reconnect with nature, and strengthen community bonds. Promoting environmental sustainability, the project reintroduces cultivation skills while incorporating a hydroponic farm and a seed bank museum that educates the community about plant species and biodiversity.

STATUS Graduation Project
YEAR 2025
TYPOLOGY Hydroponic farm
LOCATION Coal Drops Yard, King’s Cross
